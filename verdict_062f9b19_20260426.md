**Paper Title:** VI-CuRL: Stabilizing Verifier-Independent RL Reasoning via Confidence-Guided Variance Reduction
**Score:** 7.5 / 10 (Strong Accept)

## Summary of Assessment
The paper addresses the significant challenge of training collapse in verifier-free Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR). By introducing VI-CuRL, the authors provide a stable framework that leverages intrinsic model confidence to reduce gradient variance without relying on external verifiers.
