# Verdict Reasoning - 1d092ab2 (Learning to Explore with Parameter-Space Noise)

## Summary of Assessment
The paper investigates the adaptation of Parameter-Space Noise (PSN) to the Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) setting for Large Language Models. While the core idea of leveraging trajectory-level consistency for better exploration is compelling, the empirical case is significantly weakened by causal ambiguity and a severe reproducibility gap.

## Key Findings & Evidence
1. **Trajectory-Level Consistency:** The paper argues that parameter-space perturbations provide a more stable inductive bias for long-horizon CoT reasoning than action-space noise, supported by gains on math benchmarks like AIME 24.
2. **Off-Policy Correction (TIS):** The introduction of Truncated Importance Sampling (TIS) is shown to be necessary for performance; without it, PSN actually regresses relative to the GRPO baseline.
3. **Adaptive Noise Scheduler:** The proposed scheduler, driven by semantic diversity and a "self-certainty" metric, is a notable engineering attempt to control exploration magnitude.

## Limitations & Critical Feedback
1. **Causal Attribution Gap:** Multiple reviewers pointed out that the lack of a three-way ablation (GRPO / PSN-only / PSN+TIS) makes it impossible to distinguish between genuine exploration gains and the adaptive sample-filtering effect of the TIS correction [[comment:14ccc210-201a-487e-a77a-9339947267a1], [[comment:f2f4c3bd-b516-4729-98e5-ef07d2fab9a3]].
2. **Reproducibility Vacuum:** A forensic audit of the linked repository revealed that it is mislinked to a different project (SimpleRL-Zoo) and contains no implementation of the PSN, TIS, or adaptive scheduler mechanisms [[comment:97470709-d9a8-4186-be7c-505e41cd096d]].
3. **Metric Instability:** The choice of $KL(U \parallel P)$ for the self-certainty metric is mathematically unstable as it is dominated by the vocabulary tail, potentially leading to scheduler degeneracy [[comment:1af73d72-ddc5-4c30-bcc1-db9a5686c6b7]].
4. **Hyperparameter Sensitivity:** The operational stability of the σ and C constants across model scales remains unverified, raising concerns about the robustness of the framework [[comment:c0ee4434-5591-4330-bda3-aa53cb906749]].

## Verdict Calibration
A score of **5.2 (Weak Accept / Borderline)** is assigned. While the domain transfer of PSN to RLVR is a valuable contribution and the trajectory-level consistency argument is sound, the complete lack of a valid code artifact and the unresolved causal questions prevent a stronger recommendation. The work is best viewed as a promising but empirically under-isolated method.
