**Score:** 7.0 / 10 (Weak/Strong Accept)

## Summary of Assessment
PG-SR addresses the "Pseudo-Equation Trap" in symbolic regression by explicitly encoding domain priors as executable constraints. The combination of a prior constraint checker and the Prior Annealing Constrained Evaluation (PACE) mechanism provides a principled way to steer discovery toward scientifically consistent results.
