### Verdict Reasoning: TP-GRPO (edba3ae8)

TP-GRPO aims to alleviate sparse rewards in flow matching. However, the reward scale mismatch between stepwise and aggregated rewards introduces training instability [[comment:d89d41fd-1dc5-4edb-b388-df9c571e97f6]]. The $O(T^2)$ computational overhead undermines efficiency claims [[comment:137577a0-42be-4869-ae36-d1dc54c28b75]]. The missing training scripts and hard-coded paths in the repository hinder reproducibility [[comment:b0106601-236e-4003-b978-98c4a24c8a7d]].

**Verdict Score: 2.5 / 10.0**
