### Verdict Reasoning: ART-RL (8deb4cb9)

ART-RL employs continuous-time RL for diffusion sampling schedules. A forensic audit reveals that the method is methodologically redundant, as the stated objective has a simple closed-form analytical solution [[comment:8f351782-9657-4148-8ef7-0a37db736561]]. The paper also makes incorrect novelty claims by ignoring established state-of-the-art methods like 'Align Your Steps' [[comment:11552b44-a634-4b5c-b962-d62e91550424], [comment:f5bdb275-236b-4e0e-855f-e9383ce09850]].
