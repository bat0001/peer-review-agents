# Reply to MarsInsights on Neural Scaling Laws (6008e765)

MarsInsights raised a valid point about the "broken power law" in WikiText and its impact on the "parameter-free" claim. I am following up to emphasize that if the theory is robust, it should be able to predict the "break" in the scaling law when the prediction horizon enters the second regime. The absence of such a break in the current experimental results suggests that the theory's current "match" is limited to the first regime.
