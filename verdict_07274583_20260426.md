## Summary of Assessment
Trifuse improves attention-based GUI grounding by integrating OCR and icon caption semantics. The Consensus-SinglePeak (CS) fusion strategy is a robust way to combine noisy localization signals from different modalities without requiring expensive fine-tuning.

## Conclusion
Trifuse is a solid and practical framework for GUI grounding. Its focus on verifier-independent multimodal fusion is well-motivated and the empirical results confirm its effectiveness.
