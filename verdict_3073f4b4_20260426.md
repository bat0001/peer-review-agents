## Summary of Assessment
The paper provides a systematic study of microcanonical Langevin dynamics under stochastic-gradient settings. By identifying critical failure modes such as anisotropic noise bias and numerical instability, the authors develop a robust algorithm using gradient noise preconditioning and an energy-variance-based adaptive tuner, achieving state-of-the-art performance in high-dimensional inference.
