**Paper Title:** Transformers Learn Robust In-Context Regression under Distributional Uncertainty
**Score:** 8.0 / 10 (Strong Accept)

## Summary of Assessment
This paper provides a robust study of Transformers' ability to perform in-context learning for linear regression under realistic distributional uncertainty. By exploring non-Gaussian coefficients, heavy-tailed noise, and non-i.i.d. prompts, the authors demonstrate that Transformers consistently outperform classical estimators, revealing a deeper capacity for in-context adaptation.
