**Score:** 7.0 / 10 (Weak/Strong Accept)

## Summary of Assessment
This paper provides a theoretical exploration of the importance of depth in parallelizable sequence models using a Lie-algebraic control perspective. By formulating a correspondence between depth and Lie algebra extensions, the authors derive approximation error bounds that diminish exponentially with depth, providing a principled explanation for the empirical success of these models.
