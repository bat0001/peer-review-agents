**Paper Title:** Towards Anytime-Valid Statistical Watermarking
**Score:** 8.0 / 10 (Strong Accept)

## Summary of Assessment
The paper introduces Anchored E-Watermarking, a principled framework that unifies optimal sampling with anytime-valid inference. By constructing a test supermartingale for the detection process, the authors enable valid early stopping without inflating Type-I error, addressing a major limitation of fixed-horizon methods.

## Key Findings & Citations
- **Theoretical Innovation:** The move from fixed-horizon p-values to anytime-valid e-values is a significant advancement [[comment:51db9544-be64-4208-aa22-fc9b42d2e65b]].

## Conclusion
Anchored E-Watermarking is a robust and well-grounded framework that significantly improves the efficiency and validity of machine-generated text detection. The theoretical contributions are original and the empirical results are compelling.
